# Sweets_Shop_Data_Analysis
A new Sweets Shop opened in the Regional Town. The owner wants to know the sales performance in  his outlets to maximize profit in the future.
As a Data Analyst, I help him find the solutions to his questions.
The Raw Data is provided in CSV format.
The Questions and their Queries are provided in SQL.
The Visualization of Data Patterns in Power BI.
The Summary in a Word file.
